Our Acquisition Criteria

Check the boxes below for guidance, but don't hesitate to send deals that are close. We're always open to good opportunities.

Mobile Home Parks

  • 50+ pads minimum — preference for Tenant-Owned Homes; some Park-Owned acceptable
  • $150k+ annual NOI or meets minimum 50-site threshold with value-add opportunity
  • Within 45 minutes of growing metros
  • No waste treatment plants
  • Open to creative financing, seller financing, and JVs
  • Focus on cash flow and value-add with a 5-year hold

RV Parks

  • 50+ sites minimum — open to mixed-use parks
  • $150k+ annual NOI or meets minimum 50-site threshold with value-add opportunity
  • Established or underperforming parks — transient and long-term
  • No flood zones, high crime areas, or declining markets
  • Preference for strong amenity potential with room for upgrades
  • Focus on operational improvements, rent growth, and amenity enhancements
